Detailed Process

How we work together from first call to delivery

1

Free Discovery Call

1 Hour

No strings attached consultation to understand your project, workflow, goals, and expectations. By the end of this call, you'll receive a rough cost estimate and timeline to help you decide if we're a good fit.

What We'll Cover:

  • Your current workflow and pain points
  • Project goals and success metrics
  • Technical requirements and constraints
  • Timeline expectations and priorities
  • Communication preferences
  • Budget range and payment structure
2

Detailed Research & Final Quote

3-5 Days

If you want to proceed, I'll conduct thorough research into your project requirements and deliver a complete, final quote within one week.

Your Quote Includes:

  • Fixed project cost (no hourly billing, no hidden fees)
  • Realistic timeline with key milestones
  • Clear scope definition outlining exactly what's included
  • Technical architecture and approach
  • Payment schedule with 50% deposit requirement
  • Warranty terms and conditions

The quoted price is your final price. You know exactly what you'll pay upfront.

3

Project Kickoff

Same Day

Upon acceptance, we'll formalize the scope in a detailed agreement and begin with a 50% deposit. Payment milestones are built into larger projects to maintain healthy cash flow.

Kickoff Deliverables:

  • Signed project agreement with clear scope boundaries
  • Payment schedule confirmation
  • Communication cadence setup
  • Project milestone calendar
  • Access to necessary systems and resources
4

Development & Communication

Project Timeline

Communication style adapts to your preferences. I work according to agreed specifications while keeping you informed at your preferred level of detail.

Communication Options:

  • High involvement: Brief daily/weekly progress updates
  • Minimal involvement: Monthly calls with milestone summaries
  • Technical focus: Code progress and implementation details
  • Business focus: Feature completion and user-facing progress

All scope changes are documented and require separate quotes before proceeding. This protects both of us from misunderstandings.

5

Delivery & Warranty

Final Milestone

Full functionality testing, client acceptance, final payment, and your 1-year warranty period begins immediately.

Delivery Package:

  • Complete, tested software solution
  • Documentation and user guides
  • Source code and deployment instructions
  • 1-year warranty documentation
  • Optional operations handoff

1-year warranty covers any bugs or functionality issues as defined in our original specifications. This demonstrates confidence in my work quality and ensures you're protected.

6

Post-Launch Changes

Ongoing

Any modifications or additions after delivery are handled the same way - fixed pricing with clear scope, so you always know the cost upfront.

Scope Protection:

  • Change orders: Legitimate scope expansions handled transparently with new fixed quotes
  • Scope boundaries: Clear definition of original scope vs. additional work
  • Warranty limits: Covers functionality defects, not feature requests
7

Optional Ongoing Operations

Monthly

Choose to handle hosting and maintenance yourself, or let me manage it with a transparent monthly operational fee.

Operations Coverage:

  • Hosting costs and tool licenses
  • Operational issue resolution (storage limits, server maintenance, etc.)
  • Security updates and patches
  • Performance monitoring
  • No markup on third-party services - you pay what I pay

My Guarantees

Fixed Pricing

The price I quote is the price you pay. No hidden fees, no scope creep charges, no billing surprises.

1-Year Warranty

Any bugs or functionality defects covered for a full year after delivery. I stand behind my work.

Direct Communication

You work with me personally, not account managers. Updates on your schedule and in your preferred format.

Quality Standards

Maintainable, reliable, fast code built to last with minimal ongoing maintenance requirements.
